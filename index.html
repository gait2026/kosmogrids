About

The Process
Behind the Sound of Kosmogrids

Kosmogrids is an electronic music project rooted in the exploration of sound synthesis. Every composition begins at the oscillator level — shaping waveforms, sculpting filters, and modulating parameters to create evolving sonic landscapes.

The studio is built around a hybrid setup: virtual analog and digital synthesizers paired with modern digital processing and oldschool keyboards.

All music is available on Bandcamp in multiple formats, including lossless downloads.

Instruments

Modular synth, analog polysynths, drum machines, keyboards, phones, tape loops

Processing

Reverbs, delays, granular synthesis, spectral processing

Streaming

Bandcamp, Spotify, Soundcloud
